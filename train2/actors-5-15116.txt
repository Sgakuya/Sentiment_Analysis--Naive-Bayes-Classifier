Kirsten was exceptional actress as a child. Claudia in Interview with the Vampire was a complex adult character, and Kirsten played it better than any other person her age could have. In Little Women, she showed her acting prowess as little Amy, and was a standout in a cast that included Winona Ryder and Susan Sarandon. The tragic fact is that there are no roles for women of her age that would truly showcase her talents.
