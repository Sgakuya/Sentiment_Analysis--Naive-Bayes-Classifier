Of all the great comediennes of the 1930's, a list that would include Jean Arthur, Irene Dunne, Claudette Colbert, Myrna Loy and Jean Harlow, Carole Lombard's performance in the classic 'My Man Godfrey,' 1936, arguably puts her at the top of the list. And my God, she was gorgeous! Her early death is one of Hollywood's greatest losses.
