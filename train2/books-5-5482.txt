With this novel, John Irving returns to his earlier writing career by producing one the best of his novels. It ranks right up there with A PRAYER FOR OWEN MEANY, HOTEL NEW HAMPSHIRE and WORLD ACCORDING TO GARP. The story follows the exploits of an incredibly handsome young televison reporter who loses his hand in a bizare accident wiht a caged lion in India. THe exploits of the hand surgeon who replaces the hand, the family of the donor of the hand, and the reporter himself are intertwined in a highly amusing and fanciful tale. John Irving is a great writer, and this book can be counted among his best.
