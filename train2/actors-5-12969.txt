KST for some reason seems to draw the part of the flawed or tarnished personality, or the antihero. Notwithstanding this, she has a knack for bringing these sometimes less than desirable, or at least hard to comprehend, characters to life. Mainstream critics should have discovered her artistry a decade or so ago, but her mysteriously singular acting should help to build on the success of The English Patient and Horse Whisperer.
