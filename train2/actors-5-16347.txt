Great looks, wonderful talent, and charisma are just a few of the ingredients that make Maurice Benard the piece de resistance of General Hospital. No matter who he is acting with or what story he is put in, Maurice makes it work. He's also a fine and greatly admired person in addition to being a gifted actor. His many fans are thrilled that he has chosen to stay with General Hospital and continue to entertain us with the exploits of the ever fascinating Sonny Corinthos.
