This guy is an amazing actor. Spader is well-known for his 80's movie work where he normally played the smarmy bad guy in movies with the Brat Pack. However, his 90's and recent work is where he truly stands out. He saved the television show ,The Practice during it's final season with the character of Alan Shore and has continued the character in television's best written show Boston Legal. You owe it to yourself to check out this brilliant show and to see Spader.
