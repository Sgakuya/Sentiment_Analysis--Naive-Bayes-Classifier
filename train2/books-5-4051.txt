This novel should be required reading for people who live in the United States, and especially for Californians. It traces the lives of a migrant farm worker during the Depression. It completely relates to modern society by showing how exploitative and wasteful the capilist system is. Since practically everyone in the US eats fruit and vegatables. Americans need to recognize where there food is coming from and what migrant workers go through in order to make life easy for the middle class.
