A wonderful, sprawling story which spans three generations. Almost every character is memorable, from Luca Sleeping with the Fishes Brasi, Michael, Sonny, the Dutch Consigliare, Freddy, to the Godfather himself. This book is classic, a War and Peace set to the violence of mafia run New York. A story of immigration, family, love, tragedy, revenge - this book would be a classic even without the mafia intrigue. If you haven't read this book, it's about time you got with the program...
