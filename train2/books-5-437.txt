A great and imaginitive story. A mean Grinch who has a heart that is too small hates the happiness that the Whos of whoville feel at Christmas.He decides that he along with his trusty dog Max will steal Christmas from the Whos and they will be unhappy like he is. Of course at the end he finds that Christmas was not the gifts. food etc. it was good feelings that comes with Christmas. At the end a little accident makes his own heart bigger and it results in a happy ending for all. Dr Seuss with his wonderful stories comes up with another winner with this book.
