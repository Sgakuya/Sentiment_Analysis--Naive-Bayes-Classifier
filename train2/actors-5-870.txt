Along with his pals, Robert Deniro and Meryl Streep, Al Pacino is probably the greatest living actor today. To say Pacino is a master thespian doesn't do him justice as his performances can attest. Few can evolve into their characters with such passion and achive such layers of depth as Pacino. And Pacino has had more than his share of memorable roles. From the morose Michael Corleone of 'The Godfather' series to his character portrayals in 'A Scent of a Woman' and 'Heat', Pacino's acting ability is unquestioned. In short, Pacino is an acting legend and Hollywood icon.
