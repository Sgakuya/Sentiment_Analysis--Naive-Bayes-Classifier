Although Pacino is best known for his famous role in The Godfather trilogy, his acting ability goes far beyond that. Pacino's greatest attribute as an actor is his intense style. For proof of this, just watch Any Given Sunday. Pacino's performance was raw and intense, and completely believable. The same goes for The Devil's Advocate. His performance in that film was both chilling and intense. Recently, Pacino turned in a good performance alongside Robin Williams in the provocative thriller, Insomina. Pacino has had a long, colorful career as an actor with an amazing screen presence and an intense style.
