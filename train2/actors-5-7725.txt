Jonathan Jackson is a very talented and gifted young actor. Anyone in doubt should check out his role in the movie The Deep End of the Ocean, which also starred Michelle Pfeiffer as his character's mother. Jonathan was the leading role in a TV movie, Trapped in a Purple Haze, about cocaine addiction. His longest starring role was as Lucky Spencer (Luke and Laura's son) on the popular daytime soap opera General Hospital. Jonathan was up for the role of Anakin Skywalker in the second Star Wars prequel, and also has a brother Richard who has acted in movies, TV shows, and on the show Ally McBeal.
