Benny, a chubby rich girl meets Eve, the poor orfan who lives with the nuns, when she is just a little girl. They bond almost instantly and share their most deepest, and personal feelings. Well almost about anything. Eve refuses to discuss her rich family who abandonned her and Benny has a hard time with suspision. When they both go to college they meet up with the beautiful Nan, the handsome Jack and many many others. At the same time their village is sceeming and dealing behind closed doors... This is the first English book (litterature) that I ever read. It's a beautiful story and nothing is out of proportion. Just very simple and very entertaining.
