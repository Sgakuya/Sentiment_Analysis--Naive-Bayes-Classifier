I'm not surprised it's ranked near the bottom--highbrow, abstruse, and quite opaque to most people--it's the only existential strip I think I have ever seen. The series on Camille Paglia in the mid 90s was one of the funniest things I've ever seen in the paper. If you don't understand it I'll give you a hint--Zippy and Griffy are one person. Think Freud.
