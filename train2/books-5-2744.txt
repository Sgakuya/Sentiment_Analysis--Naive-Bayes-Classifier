I'm all for self-help and self-reliance. But as with anything that involves humanity, we are frail and have our limits. That's when prayer and God come into play. Prayer, God, and Jesus Christ provide spiritual nourishment for the soul. Balance is paramount for success, and if the system is just a teensy bit out of whack, then trouble happens and manifests itself in many ways: depression, drugs, suicide, homicide, et. al.
