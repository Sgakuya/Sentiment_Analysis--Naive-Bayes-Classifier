Jack Lemmon was adept at both comedy and drama. He appealed to everyone and acted almost up to the time of his death. Jack Lemmon was not afraid to try anything, and delved deep into his characters to bring out something deeper. Some great film of his include The Apartment, Some Like it Hot, The Fortune Cookie, The Odd Couple, Days of Wine and Roses, Glengarry Glen Ross, Missing, Save The Tiger, The China Syndrome, Grumpy Old Men and more. He won Academy Awards for Mister Roberts and Save The Tiger.
