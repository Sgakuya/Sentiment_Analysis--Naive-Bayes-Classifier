Amelia was born in 1972. In the 1980s she appeared in 3 classic British family classics. In 1985 she played Alice Liddell in Dreamchild, the story of Lewis Carroll's Alice in Wonderland. In 1987 she was Sara Crewe in A Little Princess and then Red Riding Hood in 1988. An excellent actress all around, very beautiful and with a posh speaking voice.
