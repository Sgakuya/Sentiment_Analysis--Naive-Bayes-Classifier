One of the greatest and most versatile of all actors. His best includes: The Killers, The Crimson Pirate, Come Back Little Sheba, From Here to Eternity, Gunfight at OK Corral, Sweet Smell of Success, Elmer Gantry, Birdman of Alcatraz, The Train, Atlantic City - and of course what other US star could have played with such confidence the Sicilian prince of The Leopard?
