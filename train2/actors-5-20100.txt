Gillian Anderson is a wonderful actress for many reasons. Her long, hard work on the X-Files has brought her fans, fame, and recognition. She is now working on this series for the 8th year running. Her starring role in The House of Mirth is showing everyone the variety of different characters her talents allow her to play. Everyday she trys to juggle the pressures of work, which takes up more than it's fair share of the day, and being a mother to her 6 year old daughter, who is now in school. All of her hard work has paid off, as she is now a well known face everywhere she goes, and she deserves every second of it.
