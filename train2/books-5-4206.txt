J.D. Salinger, though reputed to have an I.Q. @ just over 100, created a human being with the character Holden Caufield, the protagonist in Catcher in the Rye. Colored with Salinger's enigmatic tendencies, Catcher details the thoughts and exploits of the rebellious teenager on his path to self discovery and respectfully allows Caufield to remain a teenager with everything that's applicable.
