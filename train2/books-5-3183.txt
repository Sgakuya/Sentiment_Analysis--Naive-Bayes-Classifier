Dean Koontz is an incredible author! In Watchers, one the best he has ever written, an intelligent dog and beastly ape escape from a top secret government laboratory. The dog is just as smart as any human, though struggles to find a way to communicate with the humans who have taken him in. The monster is a killing machine, also genetically engineered by the government. Currently, his only goal is destroy the dog. Once the dog communicates with the humans, Nora and Travis, the trio find themselves in the flight of their life, running away all over the country, getting new identities, and trying their hardest to protect the dog from the government and the monster.
