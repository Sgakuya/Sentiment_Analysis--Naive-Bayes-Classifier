BE aware of a wolf in sheep's clothing. TSM goes by other names and hides behind a number of aliases. The most prominent is movers.ca. Bait and Switch seems to be their most popular tactic. In 30 plus years of moving, including 15 plus cross country moves, they are by far the WORST movers. A recent National Post article painted a fairly accurate portrait of how they work. They are a franchise company, and the master franchisor claims to have no power of his franchisees. Trying to get a dispute cleared up with head office is useless, and the office (Troonto) I dealt with was arrogant, rude, ignorant and totally dishonest. If you check out the various BBB offices across the country, you will find unresolved complaints registered against the company with most of them.
