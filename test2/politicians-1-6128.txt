Former Senator Ashcroft presents a frightening possibility as Attorney General. His extremist views on certain key issues including abortion, affirmative action, and gun control present a very real conflict if he is to hold the position as chief law enforcement officer. While the moral character of the man is not being called into question, his ability to execute his office in an unbiased fashion is. The broad coalition of groups that is forming to prevent his confirmation demonstrates how far off he is from moderate America.
