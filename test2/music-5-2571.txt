Man- unlike most people, I've always liked the Beach Boys. Brian Wilson's (an absolute genius) music is a lot deeper than most people expect, and this is the guys at their best. This is undoubtedly one of the top 5 albums of all time. All of the Boys are so talented and blend together like no one else.
