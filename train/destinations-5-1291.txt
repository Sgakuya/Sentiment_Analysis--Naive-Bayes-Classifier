Turkey is a surprisingly beautiful country with lots of ancient history. Istanbul is a city you can get lost in forever. The Hagia Sofia is very architechurally appealing. The setting of the city on the Bosphorus adds to the beauty of Istanbul. The ruins of ancient Ephesus are also incredible. Outside of maybe Italy and Greece, you really feel the history of the land through the myriad of ancient ruins; from the Hittites to the Byzantines and later even the Ottomans all left a distinctive legacy on the country. It's the variety of civilizations melding together in this land that makes the history of Turkey interesting. The landscapes are also incredible. If you love history, culture, and food and if you want to do something different, then go to Turkey.
