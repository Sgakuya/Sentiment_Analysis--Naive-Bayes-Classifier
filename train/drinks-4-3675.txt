great drink. if you're looking for a recipe, try this: 1-2 parts sweet and sour mix 1 part whiskey you may want to change this around to get the taste and strength you want dont buy sweet and sour mix from a store - it's crap. here's a good sweet and sour mix recipe: 1 part simple sugar syrup 1 part fresh lemon juice make the simple sugar syrup like this: pour 1 part water into a saucepan and bring it to a boil. when it starts to boil, pour in 2 parts sugar. turn down the heat and stir until the sugar dissolves. refrigerate the mix. when its cold, add in the lemon juice and shake well. keep it in the fridge until you're ready to use it. there's your sweet and sour mix.
