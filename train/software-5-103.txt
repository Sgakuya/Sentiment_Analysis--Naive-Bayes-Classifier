This PC-based mathematics software package offers a huge collection of problem-solving lessons in arithmetic, pre-algebra, algebra, trigonometry and hyperbolic trigonometry. The package contains hundreds thousands step by step solutions, with definitions, rules and proofs of used math formulas and theorems provided for each solution step. The lessons are presented in a logical sequence from simple to difficult. A set of ready tests is designed for children to review and reinforce their math skills. This package encourages children to learn through in-depth explanation of each solution step and repetition rather than through rote memorization. Those who stick with the package will come away with a stronger understanding of fundamental concepts, improved higher-level thinking skills and excellent skills in solving math problems. The package is suitable for middle and high school use.
