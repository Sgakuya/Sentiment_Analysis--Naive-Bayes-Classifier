If you love cities and urban sophistication, then you will positively LOVE Mexico City, which is at once bigger, grander, smellier, more crowded, and utterly more fascinating than just about any other world capital. In Mexico City you'll find more museums than any other capital city in the world, from the endless Museum of Anthropology to the many art museums dedicated to such masters as Diego Rivera, Rudolfo Tamayo, Frida Kahlo, and others. The Metro makes the city a safe and easy place to get around. Tons of big luxury hotels, trendy restaurants, and fashionable boutiques around the Polanco area. So much more...
