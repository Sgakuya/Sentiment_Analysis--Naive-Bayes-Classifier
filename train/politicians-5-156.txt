This pasty faced monarch truly presided over a golden age in English history, Will Shakespeare was busily scribbling plays, Walter Raleigh was introducing the potato to Europe and Sir Francis Drake was intrepidly circumventing the world (and nicking some Spanish gold, naturally). Good Queen Bess also presided over the defeat of the largest naval force ever created (The Spanish Armada) and was known to remark beforehand Though I have the body of a weak and feeble woman, I have the heart of a King, a King of England at that. However Elizabeth was a strong and independent woman, hence she never married, giving the reason that she was married to her country, a greater spouse than any man. I must say that England has not had such a happy marriage since.
