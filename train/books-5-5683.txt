After Tucker Case wrecks the Learjet of the owner of the Mary Jean Cosmetics, due to sex and booze, nobody will hire him as a pilot. That is, untill a strange doctor on a remote South Pacific island hires him to fly his brand new Learjet and to keep his mouth shut. With the aid of a dead WW2 Pilot, and a talking bat, Tucker gets tot he bottom of an international smuggling operation and saves an entire race of people in the process. Christopher Moore has become on of my favortie authors and this book helps establish him as the premiere humour author of our age. This book is a must read, and a page turner you will not want to put down,
