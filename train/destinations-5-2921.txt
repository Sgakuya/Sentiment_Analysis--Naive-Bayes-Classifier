Many sites to see including: the Empire State Building, Central Park, Times Square, Statue of Liberty, many cathedrals, Wall Street, and Broadway plays. Every Thanksgiving Macy holds the famous Thanksgiving parade. For children: New York Hall of Science, New York Aquarium, Childrens Museum of Manhattan, The Bronx Zoo, and The Staten Island Childrens Museum. New York City offers a great variety of foods in their restaurants from around the world. You can almost eat your way through the alphabet in ethnically diverse New York. (Afghan, Brazilian, Cuban, etc.) There is so much to do in this city, it is a beautiful and unique city, it's a place where everyone should visit if given the chance.
