What a fantastic woman Barbara Olson was! Her celebrity -- equally attributable to her sharp analytical mind and her unassailable warmth and charisma -- was certainly rising before she was killed onboard the plane that was hijacked and used to attack the Pentagon on 9-11. I hope this book gets the attention it deserves. I have only read a portion thus far, but her scrutinous look at the last days of the Clinton administration -- as Bill and Hillary sought to squeeze every last ounce of privilege they could ween from the office, disrespectful as always of the institution they were defiling -- is certainly worthwhile and much needed. As we rally around the freedoms we hold dear, let us not forget how important it is to have persons of character in high office. John Adams prayer for the White House in 1800 -- may only wise and honest men reside here -- should be taken to heart by all Americans...
