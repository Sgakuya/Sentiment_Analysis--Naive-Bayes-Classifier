The challenge of the curriculum is at a high level. You have to KNOW the subject matter to pass exams. Flexibility of the program is awesome. Solid core courses, and a good and growing choice of electives and specializations. You can even mix and match, taking some courses at distance and some on campus. This school is the opposite of many well known schools. It is very easy to get into, but you have to earn your way out. Unlike some schools with very high entrance cost and virtually no fails, ther is No Way you can buy a degree here!
