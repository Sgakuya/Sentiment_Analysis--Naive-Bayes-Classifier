As with any degree from any institution, you only get out of it what you put into it. If you need to be force fed everything you learn, as with most programs around, than perhaps you should consider a career in somthing other than management... I have attended 4 different universities for 3 different degrees. UOP was a refreshing change. They treated students like clients instead of a number. Class rooms were filled with people that actually had some experience in business rather than young students who could only immagine what the working world must be like...As a CEO of a reputable company it has been my experience that by and large our UOP graduates bring a more to the table than those who from other universities...
