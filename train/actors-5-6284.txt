Scott Bakula is a multi-talented actor who can play any role, from comedy to drama, with conviction and style. Contrast his performance as Captain Archer on Star Trek: Enterprise (UPN, Wed. 8PM eastern time) with his roles in the films Necessary Roughness and Lord of Illusions, among others. What a range of talent! Scott is also a wonderful singer and dancer, as evidenced in several Quantum Leap episodes, as well as in his Tony award nomination for Romance, Romance in 1988. In his private life, Scott is a caring, compassionate man who donates much of his time to numerous charities. He's also fiercely devoted to his family, and respectful of his fans. Scott Bakula belongs on your ratings list!
