His record at Youngstown should have forewarned doubters. Winning a national championship at the 1-AA level (or DII or DIII) is a monumental achievement. He did it more than once! In an age of flash, Coach Tressel is substance. He is right up there with Stoops or Spurrier or any of them on X's and O's. But the greatest thing he did at OSU is immediately restore (and add his own) traditions. The little things he has tried to re-establish, coming from a lesser coach, would be percieved as corn. Coming from him, it's gospel. Buckeyes everywhere have bought into his sincere let's all pull together philosophy-- IN JIM WE TRUST! (Woody's up there beaming...)
