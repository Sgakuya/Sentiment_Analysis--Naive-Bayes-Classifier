One of the most under-rated actresses, who doesn't even have to make an effort to be effective in a performance. She knows how to hold the audiences attention without taking a shallow role in a Hollywood blockbuster, she truly is the indie queen with a catalogue of quality work behind her - Kids, If These Walls Could Talk 2, The Last Days of Disco, Boys Don't Cry, Gummo, Shattered Glass, American Psycho. Chloe's very honest and natural in what she does, there are no airs or graces or any grey areas, what you see is what you get. She's comfortable in her own skin making her possess a unique style and eye for fashion without purposely trying to shock or trying too hard to make a huge effort to be different.
