It's good to see a diversion from the tired cash-flashin' bling thugs that seem to inhabit every other slot on this list. I think Tribe did a better job with Midnight Marauders, but Low End Theory is still a classic. Man, I so desperately long for a return to the days of hip-hop past, when there was still pressure on the artists to be good musicians first and worry about the image later, if at all. And what DID happen to Q-Tip? I seem to remember a line from his compatriot, Phife Dawg, from Midnight Marauders, Too many candy rappers seem to be at the top. Too much candy is no good, so now I'm closin' the shop. Hmm...
