charming Sci-fi movie about aliens who come to earth to claim some pods left years ago in the ocean. they disguise themselves as humans and rent a house near a retirement village. the aliens store these pods in a swimming pool at the rented house. some of the elderly men have been using swimming pool secretly. when they see the pods in the pool they swim anyway. Now they find that they are feeling much better and youthful. turns out these pods have some magical way of reviving youth. they bring more of their friends and spouses and all feel younger and better. they find out these fake humans are really aliens and they become friends with them. when they are invited to go back to the aliens planet they must decide it they want to stay on Earth and go back to growing old. or have erternal youth on another planet. Very sweet different kind of sci-fi movie. there are also a lot of great older actors in this movie.
