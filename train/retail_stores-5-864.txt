Eddie Bauer offers excellent quality, reasonable prices and exceptional service. We have purchased a variety of products from Eddie Bauer, including shoes, watches, sunglasses and clothing and have never been disappointed. The quality and durability of their clothing is the best. Not all of their styles are for me, but more often than not, I find a number of things that I simply love. Also, the sales people are always kind, considerate and helpful. There is never a shortage of staff, however they don't stalk you either. I am always asked nicely if I can be helped or am notified of a certain sale, but they are never pushy.
