Murder by Numbers is a solid forensic/crime thriller with a top-notch cast and decent plot. Bullock was as good as ever as the somewhat arrogant, but brilliant detective who along with a rookie partner is out to catch two teenagers who killed a young girl in the hopes of committing the perfect crime. Ryan Gosling delivers a particularly riveting performance, along with Michael Pitt. The film captures the viewers attention the majority of the time as we watch Bullock and her partner close in on the two teenagers. Overall, Murder by Numbers is a solid thriller with great suspense and an ending that brings the film to a satisfying close. It's definitely worth seeing at least once.
