Georgia Tech, also known as Point Guard University, has been a very successful college basketball program. They play in the toughest conference in college basketball, have made several Final Four appearances, making it to the Finals in 2004. Famous Yellow Jacket basketball players who have gone on to star in the NBA include Chris Bosh, John Salley, Stephon Marbury, Matt Harpring, Travis Best, Mark Price, Jarrett Jack, Kenny Anderson, and Dennis Scott just to name a few. Their current head coach, Paul Hewitt has brought a sense of respectability back to The Flats and looks to be building a solid program that should make even more Final 4 appearances in the future.
