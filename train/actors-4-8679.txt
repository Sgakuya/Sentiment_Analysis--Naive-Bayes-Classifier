What she may have lacked in range she made up for in sparkle. Did you ever notice how successful wardrobe seemed to be at matching her jewels to her eye color? Semi-cheesy, but I admit I actually thought that was kind of cool! And, even though her films and her roles in them were of the over dramatic soap opera school - what the heck, they were fun and entertaining which is more than you can say for many other things in the so-called entertainment category. No denying, Lana had the hair, the clothes, the figure and apparently the kind of troubled life that put dollar signs in the eyes of a tabloid publisher. In other words, she was everything you expect a movie star to be.
