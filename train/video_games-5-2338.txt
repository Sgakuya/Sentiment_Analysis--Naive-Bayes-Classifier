Great Game. you play as a farmer who has to restore a farm by clearing the land, and planting vegetables and flowers. And you have to try to stay on the farm and be friends with everyone in the game. The most interesting features of the game is you can get married to a girl if you are vey nice to her, and you can get a kid if you are married.There are a lot of festivals in the game, some with chances of winning. And you can enter in the Horse race {Spring,Fall} and the Dog Race {Winter only}This game has a lot of goals to complete if you want to be the top farmer of the game. This game may not be a Fighting or Action game, But it's a classic to me
