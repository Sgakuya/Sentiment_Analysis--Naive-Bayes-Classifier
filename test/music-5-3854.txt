Clapton is one of my favorite all-time musicians, and this is probably his flagship solo work. With songs like Cocaine, Wonderful Tonight, and Law Down Sally, this is almost like a Greatest Hits CD. But my favorite track is easily The Core, a long, amazing back-and-forth exchange with Marcy Levy. If you re a Clapton fan, this is one of the three must-have CDs (along with Layla and Other Assorted Love Songs and Blind Faith.
