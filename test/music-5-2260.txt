Far and away the young talent's best effort to date, with some classic covers (Good Morning Little School Girl, Matchbox) and some short-of-classic covers (Quitter Never Wins, etc.) mixed in with great blues originals (title track, Darker Side, Rack 'Em Up, etc.). I used to use this album as a measure of whether someone's who's heard the title track REALLY likes the blues or is shuckin' and jivin.' Basically, it delivers enough of the raw blues sound to elicit a wince if the blues blood ain't running deep (that may not be an issue, but it sure is if lovin' blues is on your must-have characteristics of my future spouse list ......
