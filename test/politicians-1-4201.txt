Unfortunately, Mr. Nader does not understand that by punishing the most productive people/entities in the country ( with taxes, penalties, etc.) the total standard of living will decrease for the country. Less money available to corporations and individuals to invest, means fewer jobs, and decreased consumption. On the surface, the things Ralph Nader aspires to are good and have a lot of emotional appeal. The implementation of these ideas is fraught with negative side affects.
